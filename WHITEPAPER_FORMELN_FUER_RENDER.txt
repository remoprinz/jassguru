# Formeln für PNG-Generierung

## Formel 1: Teamrating
R_{team} = \frac{R_{1} + R_{2}}{2}

## Formel 2: Erwartungswert
E_{A} = \frac{1}{1 + 10^{\frac{R_{B} - R_{A}}{1000}}}

## Formel 3: Expected Diff
Expected\_Diff = (2 \times E_A - 1) \times TotalStriche

## Formel 4: Actual Diff
Actual\_Diff = Striche_A - Striche_B

## Formel 5: Deviation
Deviation = Actual\_Diff - Expected\_Diff

## Formel 6: Score A (vollständig)
S_A = \max\left(0, \min\left(1, 0.5 + \frac{Deviation}{2 \times 7}\right)\right)

## Formel 7: Rating-Update Team
\Delta_{A} = K \cdot (S_{A} - E_{A})

## Formel 8: Rating-Update Gegner
\Delta_{B} = -\Delta_{A}

## Formel 9: K-Faktor
K = 32

## Formel 10: Rating-Update Spieler
\Delta_{Spieler} = \frac{\Delta_{Team}}{2}
